Intro to data

Data is information. Information is text, image and sound. In this way, data is a group of characters, colors, or frequencies.
That is how we, human beings, comprehend information. Data.

For computers, data is a group of numbers. Numbers contained in the binary system.
The binary system is a universe of two different numbers, only. Zero and one.
So, computers can only comprehend information translated to zeros and ones, to binary.
To communicate with computers, humans must translate (convert) their data to the binary system.
